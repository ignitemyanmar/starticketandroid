March

  • Peasants' Day a gazetted holiday celebrated on 03-Mar.
  • Maw-din Pagoda Festival, Ngapudaw, near Pathein celebrated on 03-Mar to 17-Mar.
  • Pakhan Ko Gyi Kyaw Festival, celebrated on 03-Mar to 17-Mar.
    Venue: Shwe Gu Ni Village (Between Pakokku and Mingyan)
  • Shwe Saryan Pagoda Festival celebrated on 03-Mar to 17-Mar.
    Venue: Shwe Saryan Village, Patheingyi Mandalay
  • Indawgyi Festival in Hopin, near Myitkyina celebrated on 10-Mar to 17-Mar.
  • Zalun Pyidawpyan Image Festival near Pathein celebrated on 11-Mar to 17-Mar.
    Venue: Zalun
  • Baw-gyo Festival near Shipaw celebrated on 12-Mar to 17-Mar.
  • Nyan Taw Pagoda Festival, (Shwe Myin Tin Pagoda) Pyin Oo Lwin (Maymyo) celebrated on 16-Mar to 17-Mar.
  • Katku Pagoda Festival, Katku celebrated on 16-Mar to 18-Mar.
    Alms offering to the monks by the native (Pa-O) people dressed in their traditional black outfits. A traditional market festival to enjoy.
  • Pindaya Cave Festival, Pindaya celebrated on 16-Mar to 18-mar.
  • Tabaung Fullmoon Day, The whole country.
  • Armed Forces Day, The whole country celebrated on 27-Mar.
